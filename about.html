About me

I haven’t had the most traditional of paths into UX Design.

For a few years I was a teacher in a bilingual school.

After that I was a DJ travelling all over the world.

Most recently I was a Mac Genius at Apple.

Sure, I wasn’t whiteboarding for a design team, but that’s because I was whiteboarding to help students connect ideas and understand concepts. I wasn’t conducting a remote usability test, because instead I was studying in-person crowd reactions of my DJ set. I might not have been creating personas to empathize with users, but that’s because I was actually empathizing with actual people as part of the Genius Bar.

No matter the task, people have always been at the center of what I do. As a designer, I love to solve problems. But my ultimate goal in UX design is find ways to help people solve problems for themselves by making my products intuitive, simple and fun to use.
